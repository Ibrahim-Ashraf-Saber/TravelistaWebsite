The most famous tourist cities

  1. Cairo
    • The Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx, the Egyptian Museum, Khan El Khalili Bazaar, Cairo Tower.
  2. Luxor
    • Karnak Temple, Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, Colossi of Memnon.
  3. Aswan
    • Philae Temple, High Dam, Nubian Village, Botanical Island.
  4. Sharm El-Sheikh
    • Diving in the Red Sea (Ras Mohammed Nature Reserve), desert safaris, relaxing on sandy beaches.
  5. Hurghada
    • Snorkeling, scuba diving, visiting Giftun Islands, enjoying luxury resorts.
  6. Alexandria
    • Diving at the Blue Hole, windsurfing, camping at Blue Lagoon.
  7. Dahab
    • Diving at the Blue Hole, windsurfing, camping at Blue Lagoon.
  8. Marsa Alam
    • Swimming with dolphins, visiting Hankorab Beach, diving at Samadai Reef.
  9. Siwa Oasis
    • Mountain of the Dead, Temple of Amun, Salt Lakes, Cleopatra’s Spring.
  10. Ain Sokhna
    • Relaxing on beaches, boat trips.
