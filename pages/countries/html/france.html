The most famous tourist cities

  1. Paris
    • Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Arc de Triomphe, Montmartre district.
  2. Nice
    • Promenade des Anglais, beaches of the French Riviera, Matisse Museum, Old Town.
  3. Marseille
    • Old Port of Marseille, Notre-Dame de la Garde Basilica, Frioul Islands.
  4. Lyon
    • Old Town, Basilica of Fourvière, Museum of Fine Arts, Presqu’île District.
  5. Cannes
    • Cannes Film Festival, shopping on Rue d'Antibes, enjoying sandy beaches.
  6. Strasbourg
    • Strasbourg Cathedral, Petite France district, European Parliament.
  7. Bordeaux
    • Place de la Bourse, Cité du Vin (Wine Museum), Dune of Pilat.
  8. Toulouse
    • Saint-Sernin Basilica, Canal du Midi, Capitole Square.
  9. Montpellier
    • Place de la Comédie, Jardin des Plantes, Triumphal Arch.
  10. Chamonix
    • Climbing Mont Blanc, taking the cable car to Aiguille du Midi.
