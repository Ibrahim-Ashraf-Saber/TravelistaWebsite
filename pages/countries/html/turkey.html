The most famous tourist cities

  1. Istanbul
    • Duden Waterfalls, Old Town (Kaleici), Konyaalti Beach, Hadrian’s Gate.
  2. Antalya
    • Duden Waterfalls, Old Town (Kaleici), Konyaalti Beach, Hadrian’s Gate.
  3. Cappadocia
    • Hot air balloon rides, exploring fairy chimneys, underground cities (Kaymakli and Derinkuyu).
  4. Izmir
    • Clock Tower, Ancient City of Ephesus, Kordonboyu, Sirince Village.
  5. Bodrum
    • Bodrum Castle, Marina, Mausoleum of Halicarnassus, sandy beaches.
  6. Trabzon
    • Sumela Monastery, Uzungol Lake, Hidirnebi Plateau.
  7. Ankara
    • Anitkabir (Ataturk’s Mausoleum), Ankara Castle, Museum of Anatolian Civilizations.
  8. Fethiye
    • Butterfly Valley, Blue Lagoon (Oludeniz), exploring Kayakoy ruins.
  9. Marmaris
    • Boat tours, exploring nearby villages, relaxing at Icmeler beaches.
  10. Bursa
    • Mount Uludag, Grand Mosque, Ottoman Village (Cumalikizik).
